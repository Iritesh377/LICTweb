About US

Lumbini ICT Campus is one of the leading reputed and renowned institution well known for never ending dedication and devotion to quality education and a candid temple of knowledge and wisdom. It is designed to disseminate quality education within the quality framework. It aims to help the students make the most of themselves in terms of academic and social perspectives. Lumbini ICT Campus caters bachelor level of program on BSc. CSIT, BIM, BCA and BHM.

It has well managed administrative staff with highly qualified experienced teachers. It is guided by dedicated management committee in conformity with the national educational goal. The college is committed to provide quality education at reasonable cost and decentralize the teaching of IT education. Determined to play a leading part in the development of modern higher education on science and technology. The members of the management board lay particular emphasis on their relationship with public bodies, other educational institutions and international ICT partners.

VISION

Lumbini ICT Campus envision that every student has his/her own potential and we ensure that he/she is provided with every opportunities to a maximum use of it. Lumbini ICT Campus is intended to develop a center of excellence for technical education and research center.

MISSION

To develop Lumbini ICT Campus a distinct college by implementing high tech education system in order to produce skilled human resource required for technologically advance world.

COMMITMENT

To offer an environment to produce skilled human resource capable of competing locally and globally.

Courses Offered:

  1. B.Sc. CSIT ( Bachelor in Science. Computer Science and Information Technology)
  2. BIM ( Bachelor of Information Management )
  3. BCA ( Bachelor of Computer Application )
  4. BHM ( Bachelor of Hotel Management)
